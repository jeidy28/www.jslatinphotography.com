Dubai 2022

July 7, 2022By JeidyJournal

What a great experience I had in Dubai this year. I am grateful for the opportunity given to travel with some amazing people from around the world. Dubai is a must visit country on your bucket list. So many things to do that you might find the time short to enjoy the country. It is a safe place to visit and the food was amazing. The beach was beautiful and clean and all the restaurants around had a great food. I am already planning to go back next year again to keep learning more of their culture.
